Breakfast Tacos

Breakfast of champions. Grab and go, freeze ahead, order through drive thru.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon (or meat of choice)
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook bacon in frying pan.
  2. Add eggs and s&p.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Heat up tortillas.
  5. Spoon egg mix into tortillas.
  6. Enjoy!
