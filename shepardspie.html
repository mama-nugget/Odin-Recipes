Shepard's Pie

A delicious dish for any day of the year


When you're running low on groceries and need a quick meal to put together, nothing better to make than Shepard's Pie. This delicious meal consists of ground beef, some mixed veggies, topped with deliciously creamy mashed potatoes. It makes great leftovers for the following day's lunch.


Base

  • ground beef
  • beef broth
  • frozen mixed veggies
  • spices
  • worcester sauce

Mashed Potatoes

  • potatoes
  • sour cream
  • spices
  • butter
  • cream cheese


Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Brown the ground beef.
  3. Add spices, broth, and W. Sauce.
  4. Meanwhile, boil the potatoes.
  5. Drain potatoes.
  6. Add remaining ingredients to potatoes and mix until desired consistency.
  7. Pour base into oven proof dish and top with potatoes.
  8. Bake dish for 20 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
